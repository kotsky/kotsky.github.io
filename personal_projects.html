Drone Landing System
During my Master's studies at the university, I developed a UAV landing system for quadcopters. This feature allows self-landing of your drone on a special symbol on the ground. It means that drone can catch the target via its optical vision system and then slowly go down until its full landing on the symbol.
As the results of this project, a test drone was programmed to fly to a defined place via GPS navigation, where it recognized a prepared symbol "H" on the ground. Then, the drone did self-landing on that symbol with 93% accuracy.

Compressive strength of concrete
In this project, I tried to the attitude between compressive strength of concrete and its attributes. Keras and sklearn are in the use to complete this project. The dataset is about the compressive strength of different samples of concrete based on the volumes of the different ingredients that were used to make them.
The outcome was a built 2-layer neural network in Python with Keras with 94% model accuracy.
