My craving for flying
Once, when I was a kid, a huge plane flew above me. At that moment I asked my mum: "What is it? An iron bird?" - "No, it's a plane. People can fly on them". At that very moment I realized my craving for flying.

For the past years I have tried a lot of flying activities: from paragliding, skydiving to flying small planes and helicopters.
